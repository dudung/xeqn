$\sin\alpha - \sin\beta = 2 \cos\tfrac12(\alpha+\beta) \sin\tfrac12(\alpha-\beta)$ | $(2)$

$\cos\alpha - \cos\beta = -2 \sin\tfrac12(\alpha+\beta) \sin\tfrac12(\alpha-\beta)$ | $(3)$
